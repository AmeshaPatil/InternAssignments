About Us

At our coffee house, every cup tells a story — crafted with passion, served with warmth. We blend rich flavors and cozy vibes to create your perfect coffee escape.

Our Products

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It’s known for its rich flavor and creamy texture, topped with a smooth, velvety froth. Often enjoyed in the morning, it offers the perfect balance of bold espresso and silky milk.

Espresso (Short Black)

A rich and intense coffee shot made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans. Known for its bold flavor and velvety crema, espresso is the foundation of many coffee drinks — perfect for those who love a strong, pure coffee experience.

Double Espresso (Doppio)

A Double Espresso, or Doppio, is a bold and intense shot made with twice the amount of coffee as a regular espresso. Perfect for those who love a rich, concentrated caffeine kick.

Short Macchiato

"A Short Macchiato is a bold espresso shot 'stained' with a small amount of steamed milk or foam. Perfect for those who enjoy a strong coffee flavor with a smooth finish."

Long Macchiato

A Long Macchiato is a double shot of espresso 'stained' with a dash of steamed milk or foam. Bold, rich, and aromatic — perfect for those who enjoy a strong coffee with just a hint of creaminess.

Ristretto

Ristretto is a short shot of espresso made with finely ground beans and less water, resulting in a richer, more concentrated flavor. It’s bold, intense, and perfect for true coffee lovers.
